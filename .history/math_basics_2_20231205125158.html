Colour schemes are important concepts in design and digital imaging. Two common colour schemes are
RGB and CMY(K):

RGB stands for Red , Green , and Blue . It is an additive colour model used in digital devices like computer monitors, television screens, and digital cameras. In this colour scheme:

Red (R), Green (G), and Blue (B) are the primary colours.
These three colours are combined in various ways to create a wide range of colours.

When all three colours are at their maximum intensity (full brightness), they create white. When all are at their minimum intensity (no light), they create black.
